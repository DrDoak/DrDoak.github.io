Projects

New Sol

Sol is an action 2D side-scrolling game with increased emphasis on NPC interactions and relationships. It is intended to be a mix strategy options such as building alliances and relationships, with the free-flowing action of a 2D side-scroller game.

Lua

In Lua, Player 1 uses the keyboard to control Lua, a platforming character in a dangerous and chaotic world ruled by Player 2, who uses the mouse to place, drop, and spawn entities to try to stop her from reaching the end of each level. Detailed control instructions are shown at the beginning of each level!

Second Law

Arcade-like physics-focused space shooter. Focuses on defending the planet from an alien invasion, while making use of Newton's Second law to propel yourself through space.!

Boxel

Boxel is a 2D side-scrolling action game. Objects in Boxel each have special modules, which can be acquired or swapped using the modular replicator. Players can employ clever tactics by combining various modules or even altering the modules of their enemies in order to defeat them. Will you survive?
