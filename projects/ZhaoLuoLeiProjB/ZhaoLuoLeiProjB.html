You are defecting from the Galactic Empire to join the Rebellion!
Unfortunately, for now you are stranded in the middle of an Imperial Outpost right in the heard of a small city.
It is your mission to find the Imperial shuttle, and escape!

Use the W A S D keys to walk around
Us the R and F keys to activate your jetpack to float up and down
(Note: Jetpack comes equipped with a super drill to go underground)
Use the Arrow keys to look around
You can resize the window to readjust the screen size

In addition, you are equipped with a personal deployable Airplane!
Press the "Space" key to deploy the plane.
In this form, you can speed up and slow down using the W and S keys.
You can also roll slightly using A and D
Changing your view will make you fly in that direction.

Press "space" again to stop Airplane mode
